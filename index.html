Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 12, 2026

YOYO Log is a paid time-tracking application. We respect your privacy and store only the information required for the app to function.

Information Stored

  • Clock-in and clock-out times
  • Break durations
  • Job or task names created by the user
  • User-provided name (optional, used for export reports)

Data Storage

All data is stored in the user’s private iCloud account using Apple CloudKit.

  • Data is associated only with the user’s Apple ID.
  • The developer does not access, view, or store user data on external servers.
  • No data is sold, shared, rented, or transferred to third parties.

Payments

YOYO Log is sold through Apple’s App Store. Apple processes payments. We do not receive or store payment details.

Analytics & Tracking

YOYO Log does not use advertising frameworks, third-party analytics, or tracking technologies.

Data Deletion

You can delete your data by removing the app from your device and/or managing iCloud storage settings under your Apple ID.
