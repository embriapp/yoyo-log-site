Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 12, 2026

YOYO Log is a paid time-tracking application. Your privacy is respected and protected.

Information Collected

  • Clock-in and clock-out times
  • Break durations
  • Job or task names created by the user
  • User-provided name (optional, used for export reports)

YOYO Log does not collect email addresses, phone numbers, location data, contacts, device identifiers, or any sensitive personal information.

Data Storage

All user data is stored securely in the user’s private iCloud account using Apple CloudKit.

  • Data is associated only with the user’s Apple ID.
  • The developer does not access, view, or store user data on external servers.
  • No data is sold, shared, rented, or transferred to third parties.

Payments

YOYO Log is a paid app distributed through Apple’s App Store. All payment processing is handled entirely by Apple. The developer does not receive or store payment details.

Analytics & Tracking

YOYO Log does not use advertising frameworks, third-party analytics services, or tracking technologies.

Data Deletion

Users may delete their data by:

  • Deleting the app from their device, and/or
  • Managing iCloud storage settings under their Apple ID.
